Biography

I did my undergrad at University of Waterloo, and currently doing MASc at Queens in Computer engineering, specifically computer vision. My research is focused on extracting depth extremely quickly from a projector and camera system. The ultimate goal of this research is to create an interactive projector system, where the projector is able to project onto dynamically changing objects.
